Technology

How it works.

Three steps from pocket to precision. Each one automated.

01  —  Scan

9-station guided LiDAR capture

The app guides you through nine positions around the foot — top, heel, left, front, right, and the diagonals in between. Each station captures a burst of LiDAR depth frames. Total scan time: under two minutes.

02  —  Process

Parametric model fitting

A deformable foot template (FIND, University of Cambridge) is fitted to the LiDAR point cloud. The model adjusts to match the exact shape of the foot while maintaining anatomically correct topology.

03  —  Measure

Automatic measurement extraction

All standard measurements are extracted directly from the fitted model — foot length, ball width, heel width, ball girth, and EU sizing. No manual measurement needed.
